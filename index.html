About Republic City

Republic City was built by notable figures such as Avatar Aang, Firelord Zuko, and Sokka of the Southern Water Tribe. Republic City is a place where people of all the nations, benders and non-benders, can live together in harmony.

Republic City is the capital of the United Republic of Nations and governed by a council. The land city is built on what was once a Fire Nation colony in the Earth Kingdom. The city has a great reputation of being one of the most modern cities in the world.

Things to do

Republic City boasts a wide variety of attractions for you to experience during your time here. The list below includes some of the most popular destinations:

  • Pro Bending Arena
  • Air Bender Island
  • Spirit Wilds
  • City Hall
  • Downtown
  • Aang Memorial Island

How we are serving you

Republic City is determined to serve you and you visit or even move here. The city has a world-renowned police force that is determined to keep you safe. Founded by the great Toph Beifong, now run by her daughter Lin, the best metal benders in the world gather here to train and serve the people of Republic City.
